OPEN PATHWAY ACCREDITATION

What is Open Pathway Accreditation?

At Open Learning College, we’re excited to introduce our Open Pathway Accreditation (OPA) programme. OPA is the process designed to help our educators get their courses accredited through leading awarding bodies. With OPA, our team will take your course through the accreditation process and ensure that it meets the rigorous standards set by the awarding bodies. Courses that receive Open Pathway Accreditation will be listed in our course offerings with an OPA badge and their affiliated awarding body accreditation, letting students know that they can trust the quality and validity of the course content.
