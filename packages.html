Still got a Question?

Please review our FAQ's below, if you still need help, please visit our Support Centre.

How can I make payment to subscribe?

The easiest way is by credit or debit card, alternatively for organisations we can send you an annual invoice. Monthly subscriptions must be paid by credit or debit card.

Why are certain packages free?

Our Student, Educator and Course Provider packages are free as we only make a charge when a student enrols on your course, so you are able to build and load your course for free.

What is the minimum subscription period?

There is no minimum period, we offer month to month rolling packages, cancel by giving 30 days notice. For an annual discounted package you will be committed for the full term.

Can I create a customised package?

Absolutely! Our team is happy to work with you to create a customised package that aligns with your goals. Contact us for personalised assistance.

Why can I not sign up immediately for certain package?

Our Business PRO, Institution & Charity packages will be tailored to your needs, we want to give you the best price and experience and allocate an account manager to support you.

How can I upgrade my current package?

Upgrading your package is easy. Simply contact our support team, and they will guide you through the process, ensuring a seamless transition to a more comprehensive package.
