About V.O.I.C.E

VIRTUAL – ONLINE – INNOVATIVE - CLASSROOM - EXPERIENCE

V.O.I.C.E™. is our proprietary learning platform, integrating our learner management system and live video Virtual conference in the METAVERSE for teachers and students.

It provides a centralised location for all course content, making it easy to organise and deliver high-quality education to students. Course Builder is also designed to be flexible, allowing educators to customise their courses to meet the specific needs of their students.

With this platform, educators can focus on delivering quality education without worrying about the technical aspects of course creation and management.

Distance Learning is a term used to describe a method of learning where you; the learner, are not physically required to be ‘onsite’ to be guided through learning materials. Learners and tutors will use methods such as email and telephone to communicate, with you deciding how, when and where you learn.

eDistance Learning gives you the flexibility to complete our programmes as quickly or as slowly as is convenient for you. To make this possible, our learning programmes have been written and prepared in such a way that it enables you to take control over these decisions. We also have a varied range of e-learning courses available to complement our full portfolio of distance learning courses.
