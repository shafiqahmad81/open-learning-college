Student Card

TOTUM is a student discount card provided by the National Union of Students (NUS). It is the new name for the NUS Extra card and offers more than just discounts on shopping. It is available to all students.

With a TOTUM card, students can get discounts on a wide range of products and services including travel, entertainment, fashion, tech and food and drink .. In addition to the discounts, TOTUM also offers access to exclusive competitions, giveaways, and experiences, giving students the chance to win amazing prizes and attend events. TOTUM also has a mobile app, which makes it easy for students to find and redeem discounts on the go . You can also apply for a UK Citizen ID card widely accepted for ID and age verification purposes.

TOTUM is the NUS Discount Card for Students
