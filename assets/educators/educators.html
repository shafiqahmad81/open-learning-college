About Educators Packages

The Open Learning College offers a range of tailored packages to cater to the diverse needs of individuals and organisations alike. With seven packages to choose from, including Students, Educators, Course Providers, SME, Business PRO, Nursery or School, and Charities, each one comes with a unique set of services to help achieve your goals. These services include V.O.I.C.E incorporating, Course Builder (LMS), ZOOM for Education & the Metaverse, in addition to Xperience™, Open Pathway Accreditation (OPA), a comprehensive Resource Library and Career Hub, providing you with everything you need to succeed in your learning and development journey

Benefits Of Educators Packages

Course Builder is a powerful and user-friendly platform designed to simplify the process of creating and managing courses. With Course Builder, educators have access to a wide range of features, including course creation, quizzes, surveys, assignments, and much more.

It provides a centralised location for all course content, making it easy to organise and deliver high-quality education to students. Course Builder is also designed to be flexible, allowing educators to customise their courses to meet the specific needs of their students.
