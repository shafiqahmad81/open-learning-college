06. Easy Payment Plan & Funding

Take advantage of our Easy Payment Plans which help you spread the cost of your course over a number of months – Interest Free! Simply pay a deposit now and then make four monthly payments by direct debit or debit/credit card.

Clicking on your chosen course provides an example of what your monthly payment might be, depending on the price of your chosen course. This could be as little as £1 per day invested in you and your future prospects!

Alternatively, if you prefer you can:

  • Pay in full online and take advantage of a special 20% online discount
  • If you prefer to enrol over the phone, you can benefit from a 10% discount if you pay for your course in full.
  • We aim to make learning as accessible to as many people as possible, therefore by prior arrangement we can spread your monthly payments to 6 or even 9 months, our standard payment terms are a deposit plus 4 monthly payments. Please call a course advisor or submit your proposal on our easy payment plan page.

Monthly payments are normally collected on or around a month after you pay your deposit and then the same time thereafter each month.

07. UK & International Delivery

Study Pack

If you have chosen to receive a study pack (learning materials by post), this will be despatched within 7 days of your payment being received.

Open Learning College Branded Items

28 days Post enrolment we will send you the current promotional branded item at that time for Open Learning College, along with your enrolment pack and welcome letter.

All UK deliveries are sent by Royal Mail and do not include insurance. UK orders are despatched within 7 days of the order being made (usually within 48 hours). Free Postage & Packing for UK orders when you pay online. All Orders will be sent by Royal Mail.

All International Orders (Paper Based Courses) will incur an additional charge toward Postage & Packaging. This will depend on the course you choose. Please see the Course of your choice for more details.

Please note: Payment Plans are not available to our International Students
