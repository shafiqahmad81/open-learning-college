Open Learning College has no entry requirements for our courses.

As part of our widening participation goals, we are committed to an open enrolment policy. Students need to be proficient in English in order to comply with the College’s English Language proficiency requirements.

No prior learning knowledge or experience is essential to take any of our courses unless stated otherwise in the course details.

All our courses are openly available to anyone wishing to learn more about the subject and would like to take part in a highly rewarding home study course.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to expand their knowledge and study further, so we try to keep our entry requirements to a minimum.

You have the freedom to start the course at any time and continue your studies at your own pace for a period of up to 12 months from initial registration with full tutor support.

Fees for students outside the UK or EU will generally be same as the standard fee as explained on the Enrolment Fees section of the Courses.

There will be an additional postal charge for Study Packs of £55 for all orders from the EU and an additional £80 for orders from the Rest of the world- this will be automatically added at checkout (if you are enrolling for the online version there are no extra fees).

International students must make payments in full. (Unfortunately, we will have to reject any instalment enrolments made from outside the UK.) Please note that all fees must be paid in pounds sterling (£) and not in any other currency.
